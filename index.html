History of Feastable

Launched by MrBeast in January 2022, Feastables offers high-quality, ethically-sourced chocolates. The brand quickly gained popularity through MrBeast’s influence, focusing on clean ingredients and sustainability. Feastables combines excellent taste with philanthropic efforts, expanding its range while aligning with MrBeast’s commitment to quality and social responsibility..

Ingredients

Feastable typically consists of milk, sugar, cocoa solids, cocoa butter, and emulsifiers like lecithin. The main ingredient is milk, which provides creaminess and flavor, while sugar adds sweetness. Cocoa solids and cocoa butter contribute to the chocolate flavor and texture. Emulsifiers help ensure a smooth consistency, making Feastable a beloved chocolate choice.
